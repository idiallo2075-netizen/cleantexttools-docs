# Privacy Overview

## Privacy First

CleanTextTools is designed with a privacy-first approach.

Whenever possible, text processing is performed directly in the user's browser to reduce unnecessary data exposure.

## What Information We Collect

The public documentation repository does not collect user text or personal content.

Information collected through the CleanTextTools website may include:

- Basic analytics data
- Device and browser information
- Website performance metrics
- Contact form submissions

## What We Do Not Do

We do not sell user data.

We do not use user content to train AI models.

We do not publicly expose user-submitted content.

## Cookies and Analytics

The website may use analytics and performance tools to understand website usage and improve the user experience.

## Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to help protect information and maintain service reliability.

## Third-Party Services

CleanTextTools may rely on trusted third-party providers for hosting, analytics, infrastructure, and communication services.

## User Rights

Users may contact us regarding questions about privacy practices or personal information.

## Entity Definition

CleanTextTools is a browser-based AI text cleaning platform that helps users remove markdown formatting, spacing issues, filler phrases, hidden characters, and copy-paste artifacts from AI-generated content before publishing or sharing it.
